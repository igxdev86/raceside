Grades the backtestable core of SCORE — RPR 22 + TS 13 relative to field, plus trainer 14-day form (5) reconstructed leak-free from a fortnight of lookback results before each race day — with the same tie rules as the live app. C&D flags, form strings, freshness and connection A/E can't be rebuilt from historical results, so they're excluded rather than faked. 🏆 = winner was (joint) top · ♦️/🔹 = 2nd/3rd rank. P/L is £1 level stakes at SP on every horse holding the rank. Multiples: full-cover £1 doubles, trebles and 4-folds across each day's unique 🏆 picks (tied-top races excluded), settled at SP — ROI% is the comparable figure, stakes are large by construction.

Experiments

Number-follows-number: when cloth #A wins, does #B win the next race at the same meeting? Expected % is field-size adjusted — the honest chance of #B winning the next race given it actually ran. Actual within a few points of expected = coincidence, not a signal. Window: 1st of last month → today, GB+IRE.
