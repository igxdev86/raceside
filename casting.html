Ordered combinations of saddle-cloth numbers filling the first two (forecast) and first three (tricast) places, GB & IRE, from 1 January 2026. Low numbers will dominate — in handicaps cloth 1 is the top-rated horse, so 1→2 leading the board is the ratings system talking, not magic numbers. The interesting rows are the ones that punch above what the numbers' individual win rates predict. Races without valid cloth numbers in the frame are excluded. Avg SP = mean decimal SP of each finisher in that combination · SP× = mean product of those SPs, a rough what-it-paid proxy (real CSF/tricast dividends use their own formulas and typically pay less).
